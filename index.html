01The Concept

What is OSINT?

OSINT stands for Open-Source Intelligence: collecting and connecting publicly available information from leaks, profiles, records, metadata, search engines, and other open sources.

⌕

It is the same practice used by journalists, cybersecurity teams, recruiters, moderators, and everyday people who need to verify what is visible in public data.

The data is scattered everywhere. Analysis focuses on connecting the dots quickly.
